About Us

Dhabiti House Constructors Online Service is a web based platform that helps customers reach house builders and constructors, specifically masons, plumbers, electricians and painters for their services. The need for this platform arises from the fact that it is quite difficult to find any of these workers when needed, especially in urban centers. Many of these builders work through referrals which is a really uneven way of job distribution. This way, it limits the amount of customers the constructor handles. When customers do find a worker, it is challenging to verify whether or not the he or she is well-qualified for the job.
